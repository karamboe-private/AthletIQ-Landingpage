AI Injury Risk Prediction

Machine learning models analyze biometric, training load, and historical data to calculate each player's live injury risk score — before problems arise.

Player Form Tracking

Monitor every player's physical condition and performance trend in real time. Know who's peaking and who needs rest before it affects the game.

Full Squad Overview

Smart Alerts

Device Integration
