Integrations

Seamless data integration for every club.

Our platform brings wearable and GPS data together in one place, while AI modules analyze that information to give coaches and performance staff a complete view of every player’s readiness.

Fast wearable sync

Connect watches, Whoop bands, rings, and other wearables for live readiness data.

Reliable GPS APIs

Pull session and match metrics from suppliers like Catapult and STATSports.

Smart AI analysis

AI modules process the data to deliver predictive insights and meaningful player signals.

1. Wearables integration

Any wearable device can connect to AthletIQ. We support watches, Whoop bands, smart rings, and other wearable sensors. Data from these devices is synced into the system in real time so you can monitor player load, recovery, and readiness continuously.

2. GPS integration

GPS suppliers such as Catapult and STATSports are integrated through APIs. We bring session and match tracking data into the platform automatically so every movement profile, speed report, and distance metric is available after the activity.

  • Post-session data sync for easy review
  • Match tracking visible in clean dashboards
  • Combine GPS metrics with wearable and AI insights

3. AI integration

Multiple AI modules are integrated into the system. These AI layers process, analyze, and interpret the incoming data so your staff gets the best insights available. We use different AI providers together to deliver the most accurate, robust results.

  • AI-driven injury risk scoring
  • Performance pattern detection
  • Adaptive recommendations based on club data
